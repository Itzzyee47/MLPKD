# MLPKD
## MLPKD — ML-Powered Kidney Disease Prediction Platform

MLPKD is a web-based clinical decision support system that leverages machine learning to enable early detection and management of Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD). The platform integrates a trained predictive model — built on patient lab values and vitals (creatinine, urea, hemoglobin, albumin, specific gravity, sodium, blood pressure, etc.) — into a role-based healthcare portal accessible to doctors, nurses, lab technicians, and patients.

### Key capabilities include:

CKD risk prediction from routine lab results using a trained ML classifier
Role-specific dashboards for streamlined clinical workflows
Patient record management with longitudinal prediction tracking
Lab result submission and flagging for abnormal kidney panel values
Vitals recording and monitoring by nursing staff
By bringing ML inference directly into the clinical interface, MLPKD reduces the diagnostic burden on clinicians and supports earlier, data-driven intervention for at-risk patients — particularly in resource-limited healthcare settings where specialist access may be delayed.
